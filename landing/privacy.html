Who we are

CareerPulse is operated by ACAS-Apps. We are the data controller for the personal data you provide.

Special note on clearance

Security clearance is sensitive. If you choose to share your clearance level (BPSS, SC, DV) for salary benchmarking, it is stored in an encrypted column, used only to adjust your benchmark, and is never shared in any commercial dataset — even in aggregated form.

What we collect, with your consent

  • Salary lookups — role / location / clearance you check.
  • CV scoring — only the score breakdown leaves your device. The raw CV text never does.
  • Job alerts — skills / sectors / locations you save.
  • Skills research — which skills you look up.
  • Aggregated analytics — opt-in. Anonymised, combined with others' data, then included in hiring-market reports we sell.

Lawful basis

Consent (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). Withdraw any time in-app or by emailing privacy@acas-apps.example.

Your rights

Access, correct, port, restrict, or delete your data — email us and we respond within one month.

Retention

Raw event records: 12 months. Aggregated, anonymised statistics: kept indefinitely.
